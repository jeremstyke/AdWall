📌 About THE WALL

A free community ad board — pin it, vote it, share it.

AdWall is a place where anyone can pin a banner ad for free — no account, no credit card, no gatekeeping. It's a community-driven wall: the ads people care about rise to the top, the rest fade out.

📌 Posting an ad

Upload an image, add a link to your site, pick a country (or Worldwide), and you're live. One ad per day per person, to keep the wall fair.

⬆️⬇️ Voting

Everyone can upvote or downvote any ad — one vote per ad every 24 hours. Switch between Top (most voted) and New (most recent) to browse the wall.

🔗 Sharing = free votes

Every ad has a share link. When someone new opens your ad's shared link, it automatically earns a free upvote — no extra clicking needed on their end. Share it with friends, on social media, wherever — it's the easiest way to climb the rankings.

⭐ Featured ads Sponsored

Want guaranteed visibility? Feature your ad for $5 / 7 days. Featured ads show in their own strip at the top of the wall, separate from the organic rankings — so the community's picks stay honest either way.

🌍 Country targeting

Pick a specific country when you post, or go Worldwide. Visitors can filter the wall to see ads for their region, or browse everything.
