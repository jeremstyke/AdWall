Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

THE WALL is a free community ad board. This page explains what data is collected and how it's used.

What we collect

When you submit an ad, we store: the image you upload, the destination link, an optional name/brand, and the country you choose to target. No account or email is required to post an ad.

Voting

Votes are tracked locally in your browser (localStorage) to limit you to one vote per ad every 24 hours. This information isn't linked to your identity and never leaves your device except as an anonymous vote count.

What we don't collect

We don't use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or collect personal information such as your name, email, or IP address through the site itself.

Content removal

If you'd like an ad removed, contact us — see the Support page.
